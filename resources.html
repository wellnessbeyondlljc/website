You Can Help

Know the signs

Warning signs may include

  • Talking about feeling hopeless or being a burden
  • Withdrawal from friends, family, or activities
  • Significant changes in sleep or mood
  • Increased anxiety, anger, or emotional distress
  • Giving away possessions or saying goodbye
  • Expressing thoughts of self-harm or suicide

You can help save a life

  • Listen without judgment
  • Take concerns seriously
  • Encourage professional support
  • Stay connected and check in
  • Know local and crisis resources
  • Remind others they are not alone
